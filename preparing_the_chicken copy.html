Preparing the Chicken

This step is to ensure the chicken skin is sufficiently dry, and the proteins broken down enough to form micro blisters, which further crisp the wings. j

Estimated time is 3 hours minimum and overnight ideally.

In order to dehydrate the skin and break down tough proteins in the skin we will treat them with baking soda.

  1. Open the package of chicken and dump into a large bowl
  2. Add the baking powder, kosher salt, and cornstarch to the bowl
  3. Toss the chicken and dry ingredients until the wings and drummies are evenly coated
  4. Space the pieces of chicken evenly on a well greased wire rack set within a baking pan
  5. Place in the refriderator - uncovered - for at least 3 hours and up to overnight.
