Baking and Finishing

Chicken wings should be dry to the touch and have a faint, white glaze on them.

20 - 35 min in 450 degree oven.

You should notice that the baking powder treatment has created numerous micro-blisters on the outside of the wings. These will add to the crispy shatter of your finished wings.

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees, and turn on the convection baking option if you have it.
  2. Place chicken on the middle rack of the oven and cook for 20 - 25 minutes
While the chick wings cook, give your glaze a stir to reincorporate any separation.
  1. Remove the wings from the oven and toss in sesame oil and soy sauce
  2. Toss in rub seasoning until evenly coated
  3. Return the wings to oven and Broil for an additional 3 minutes per side
  4. Remove wings and let cool for a minimum of 5 minutes
  5. Plate up and enjoy!
