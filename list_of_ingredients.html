List of Ingredients

This recipe calls for the following ingredients. Adjust to taste as you wish!

Chicken

We recommend both drummies and wings, or "flats", as they are sometimes called.
  • 2 lbs. packaged chicken wings and drummettes.
    • Preferrably Air-chilled
  • 2 tps. baking powder
  • 2 tps. corn starch
  • 2 tps. kosher salt

Jerk Rub

  • 1 ½ Tbs. freshly ground allspice
  • 2 Tbs. dried thyme
  • 1 tsp. ginger powder
  • ½ tsp. cinnamon
  • ½ tsp. freshly ground clove
  • ¼ tsp. freshy ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp. lime salt
  • ¾ Tbs. garlic powder
  • 1 Tbs. pink himalayan sea salt
  • 3 Tbs. dark brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs. white granulated sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp. freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1 Tbs. dried chives
  • 1 Tbs. scotch bonnet powder
  • 1 tsp. sesame oil
  • 1 tsp. dark soy sauce

Baking Pan and Rack

You will also need a baking pan with compatible wire rack. This is a must for crispy chicken, as it will keep the wings from steaming in their own juices.

Substitutions

You can substitute any of the fresh ground spices for their pre-ground counterparts - but fresh is always better!

Scotch bonnet can be difficult to find; cayenne or habanero powder will work well as a substitute. You can always add red chili flake as well.

You may substitute ssesame oil for any oil of your choosing. You may also substitute dark soy sauce with regular.
