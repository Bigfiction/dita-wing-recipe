Preparing the Jerk Glaze

This is not a marinade.

20 minutes prep time.

Ideally, this glaze will sit overnight in order for the flavors to get to know each another.

  1. Grind your whole spices first: clove, allspice, black pepper
  2. Combine the fresh ground spices and all other dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Mix thouroughly
  4. Add oil and stir until a thick glaze forms
  5. Set aside if you using same day, or refridgerate overnight
